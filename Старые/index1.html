Project: Military × Japanese Garden

Metamorphosis: From aggression to harmony. A soldier's tent, overgrown with moss, becomes a kimono.

Conflict

Military: Function, rigidity, aggression.
Garden: Flow, contemplation, organic decay.

Color & Material

Earthy greens, stone greys. Raw, unbleached linen-cotton blends. Fabrics that age with a personal patina.

Silhouette & Details

Kimono-inspired cuts with uninterrupted lines. Hidden functionality, low-contrast embroidery, stone-like buttons.

Narrative

For the 'post-strategic' individual. Inner resilience over external dominance. A wardrobe as a personal artifact.

Project: Cyberpunk × Soviet Brutalism

Post-Soviet Techno-Asceticism. Where high technology doesn't shout, but whispers from behind concrete.

Conflict

Cyberpunk: Neon, glare, saturation.
Brutalism: Concrete, silence, monumentality.

Color & Material

Raw concrete greys, oxidized metal. Rough leather, technical fabrics with a 'used' look. Holographic patches with low contrast.

Form & Details

Heavy, geometric silhouettes. Cubist-inspired fasteners. Internal lacing, hidden tech. Functionality as architecture.

Narrative

Technology as archaeology. The future that looks like the past. For those who see utility as the highest form of aesthetics.
